About Us

Wayout solution is a company that has created a brand concept consisting of both skin care, health & beauty products by utilizing multiple channels of distribution. Experienced in sourcing, marketing & distributing skin care and beauty products from both the domestic and overseas markets. We mainly import branded beauty products from Europe and other Asian countries and distribute them through our expert distribution channels.

Wayout Solution at a glance

  • Import color cosmetics, skin care products, Shampoo, Baby Food etc.
  • Experienced management from various field in import-export, brand development, Sales & distribution
  • Strong Field Force & Distribution Network All over Bangladesh
  • 3 warehouses, Field force coverage in 5 divisions (out of 8)
  • Currently operating in major cities with Approx. 649 Wholesale outlets & Approx 3,000 retail outlets
